Most carbon footprint calculators use global averages that don't accurately reflect Indian household consumption patterns. EcoTrack was built specifically for the Indian context, with emission factors calibrated to India's energy grid, transportation infrastructure, and dietary habits.

Why India-Specific Factors Matter

India's carbon intensity differs significantly from Western countries. Our electricity grid mix (coal-heavy but rapidly greening), public transit usage, and predominantly plant-based diet mean generic global calculators can significantly over- or underestimate your actual footprint.
