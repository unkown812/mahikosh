Annual Commute Emissions Comparison

For a 10 km daily commute (one-way, 5 days/week, 48 weeks/year):

  • Car: 816 kg CO₂/year — equivalent to 408 vegetarian meals
  • Bus: 384 kg CO₂/year — 53% less than car
  • Train: 192 kg CO₂/year — 76% less than car
  • Bike/Walk: 0 kg CO₂/year — 100% emission-free

Tips for Reducing Commute Emissions

  • Switch to public transit 2 days per week to cut emissions by 40%
  • Walk or bike for trips under 5 km — zero emissions and max EcoBucks
  • Carpool with colleagues to reduce per-person emissions by up to 75%
